# Code of Conduct

We want this project to be useful and welcoming to every student and contributor.

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Do not post spam, referral links, scraped personal information, or misleading listings.
- Protect candidate privacy and never include applicant data in issues.
- Disagree with ideas without attacking people.

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when needed to protect the community and the accuracy of the board.

Report sensitive concerns privately at support@applyguy.ai.
